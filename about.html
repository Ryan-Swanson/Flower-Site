About

This is in a way a passion project for me. I am a lover of all things colorful, especially naturally vibrant. I chose to make a website about flowers, because I felt that they were so variant and beautiful, that there would be no way I could possibly run out of content, and it would also allow me the opportunity to make a site that captures beauty thats all around us, that we may not see, or think about these days.
