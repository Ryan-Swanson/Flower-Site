What are flowers?

A flower is the reproductive structure found in blooming plants. Flowers will generally facilitate reproduction, which sometimes leads to outcrossing (fusion of sperm and eggs from different individuals in a population), resulting from cross polination or self polination.

Colors

Many flowering plants reflect as much light as possible within the range of visible wavelengths. Flowers that reflect the full range of visible light, and are generally perceived as white. Color allows a flowering plant to be selecive about the pollinator it seeks to attract. Some flowering plants also have a limited ability to modulate areas of absorption.
