Symbolism

Many flowers have important symbolic meanings in Western culture. The practice of assigning meaning to flowers is known as floriography. Some common examples are Red roses given as a symbol of loves, poppies as a symbol of consolation or to commemorate, or lilies symbolizing resurrection/life.
